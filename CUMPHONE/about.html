WHO WE ARE

The company Veslo was founded in 1995 by five friends and has since grown to a very large scale. We are creating smartphones that have the best hardware at the time of their release. We have more than 236,000 employees and operate in more than 170 countries, serving more than 5 billion people worldwide. We strive to provide digital technology to every person, home, so that everyone has access to information at any time.

OUR MISSION

We strive to convey our ideals, namely: environmental friendliness, convenience, simplicity, so that all people who live even in the most remote corners of the world can improve their daily lives and enjoy modern amenities.
